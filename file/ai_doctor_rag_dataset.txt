
AI MEDICAL RAG DATASET (BILINGUAL AMHARIC + ENGLISH)
====================================================

### DISEASE: ጉንፋን (Common Cold / Flu)
SEVERITY: Mild
DESCRIPTION: Viral infection of respiratory tract causing mild illness. የመተንፈሻ ቫይረስ ኢንፌክሽን
SYMPTOMS: fever, cough, sore throat, runny nose, fatigue
CAUSES: viruses (rhinovirus)
TREATMENT: rest, fluids, OTC medicine

---

### DISEASE: ብሮንካይተስ (Bronchitis)
SEVERITY: Moderate
DESCRIPTION: Inflammation of bronchial tubes. የመተንፈሻ መንገዶች እብጠት
SYMPTOMS: cough with mucus, chest pain, shortness of breath
CAUSES: virus, bacteria, smoke
TREATMENT: rest, fluids, antibiotics if bacterial

---

### DISEASE: የሳንባ ምች (Pneumonia)
SEVERITY: Severe
DESCRIPTION: Lung infection filling air sacs with fluid. ሳንባ ኢንፌክሽን
SYMPTOMS: fever, severe cough, chest pain, breathing difficulty
CAUSES: bacteria, virus
TREATMENT: antibiotics, oxygen, hospital care

---

### DISEASE: የልብ ድካም (Heart Attack)
SEVERITY: Emergency
DESCRIPTION: Blocked blood flow to heart. የልብ ደም ፍሰት መቋረጥ
SYMPTOMS: chest pain, sweating, nausea, breathlessness
TREATMENT: emergency hospital care

---

### DISEASE: ስትሮክ (Stroke)
SEVERITY: Emergency
DESCRIPTION: Brain blood flow interruption. የአእምሮ ደም ፍሰት መቋረጥ
SYMPTOMS: weakness one side, speech difficulty, vision loss
TREATMENT: emergency care

---

### DISEASE: ካንሰር (Cancer)
SEVERITY: Severe
DESCRIPTION: Uncontrolled abnormal cell growth. ያልተቆጣጠረ ሴል እድገት
SYMPTOMS: weight loss, fatigue, bleeding, lumps
TREATMENT: surgery, chemo, radiation

---

### DISEASE: የስኳር በሽታ (Diabetes)
SEVERITY: Chronic
DESCRIPTION: High blood sugar disorder. የደም ስኳር መጠን መጨመር
SYMPTOMS: thirst, frequent urination, fatigue, blurred vision
TREATMENT: diet, insulin, exercise

---

### DISEASE: የአልዛይመር በሽታ (Alzheimer)
SEVERITY: Chronic
DESCRIPTION: Memory loss brain disorder. የማስታወስ ችሎታ መቀነስ
SYMPTOMS: memory loss, confusion, behavior change
TREATMENT: symptom management only

---

### DISEASE: አርትራይተስ (Arthritis)

DESCRIPTION: Joint inflammation. የመገጣጠሚያ እብጠት
SYMPTOMS: pain, swelling, stiffness
TREATMENT: medication, physiotherapy

---
